Egg Bhurji

A popular indian dish that fits in with rice and roti

Egg bhurji is an easy dish to make but to make it taste in a dhaba style takes a little bit more effort

Ingredients

  • 4 to 6 eggs
  • 1 Medium sized onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 Small tomatoes (chopped)
  • 3 tablespoons coriander leaves (finely chopped)
  • 5-6 curry leaves
  • 2-3 green chillies (finely chopped)
  • 1/4 tsp. cumin seeds
  • 1/4 tsp. turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp. red chilli powder
  • 1/2 tsp. pepper powder
  • 2-3 tsp. fresh lime juice
  • Salt (to taste)

Steps

  1. Add approximately 3 tablespoons of oil to a pan set on medium heat.
  2. Add cumin seeds, curry leaves, and onions. Sprinkle some salt on the onion so it cooks faster.
  3. Stir fry the onions till they turn translucent, it should take approximately 2 minutes or so
  4. Next add chopped green chillies, turmeric powder and chopped tomatoes.
  5. Stir fry the tomatoes till they are slightly soft and add red chilli powder
  6. Give that a quick stir
  7. Turn down the heat and add in the beaten eggs
  8. When the eggs are almost solidified, add in black pepper powder and fresh lime juice
  9. Continue to stir the eggs till all the liquid evaporates
  10. Next add in half the amount of coriander leaves and stir it for some more time
  11. Add salt as needed and break up the egg chunks into smaller pieces with your spatula
  12. Garnish with coriander leaves
