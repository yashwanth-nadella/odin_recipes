Scrambled eggs

For a tasty and nutritious breakfast

The secret to making scrambled eggs is all about patience...and the proper pan.

Ingredients

  • 4 to 6 eggs
  • 4 to 6 tablespoons of milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional herbs

Steps

  1. Break the eggs and whisk them
  2. Add 1 tablespoon of milk per egg and cotinue whisking (optional)
  3. Heat a nonstick skillet with a tablespoon of oil or butter
  4. Add eggs and stir
  5. Keep turning the eggs and while adding salt and pepper
  6. When the eggs are almost cooked then turn of the heat and keep stirring
  7. Ta da! your eggs are ready!
